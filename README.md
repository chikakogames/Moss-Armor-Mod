**New moss items!**

This Mod adds an additional armor and tools.

1. Moss Armor
2. Moss part
3. Moss Tools

You can: craft moss parts with a moss block, 1 moss block give 4 moss parts. After that you can craft tools or armor with the moss parts. 

You can eat the moss part now! If you are starving in a lush cave, grab a moss, transform it into moss parts, and eat those. Its not so much, but at least you will not starve in there.

For any help, join the [Discord](https://discord.gg/KYaRtM2GaB).
